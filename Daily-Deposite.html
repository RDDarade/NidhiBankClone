Daily Deposit

DAILY DEPOSIT

Daily Deposit Scheme is a monetary deposit scheme introduced by Suvarna Laxmi Nidhi Limited to help daily wage earners, small traders, to inculcate saving habits and also as a way of funding their bigger capital requirements.

DAILY DEPOSIT SCHEME

Features

  • The minimum denomination of the scheme is Rs.100/- per day and in multiples of 100.
  • 12 to 60 months schemes
  • Cash at door facility
  • Continuing in nature. No maximum period
  • Monthly income schemes, Investment doubling schemes
  • Premature withdrawal: In case deposit opened is prematurely withdrawn/closed before tenure, the applicable penal rate will be 3%.
*Terms & condition apply and all deposit plan only for members/shareholders.
