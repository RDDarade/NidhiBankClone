FIXED DEPOSIT

A fixed deposit (FD) is a financial instrument provided by banks or NBFCs which provides investors a higher rate of interest than a regular savings account, until the given maturity date. It may or may not require the creation of a separate account. A fixed deposit is a financial instrument where a member gets assured ROI.

Features

  • Attractive interest rates
  • Special rate for senior citizens
  • Loan facility up to 80%
  • Premature withdrawal: In case the deposit is prematurely withdrawn/closed before tenure, the applicable penal rate will be 4%
  • Joint account can be opened
  • Short term and long-term deposits
  • Monthly income schemes, Investment doubling schemes

Terms & Conditions

  • Minimum FD would be of Rs.10,000 and further in multiples of Rs.1,000
  • Interest calculation is based on simple compound interest
  • Loan available against deposit up to 95% of deposited amount after one month from the date of deposit
  • Pre-Mature Closure of Deposits:
    • Foreclosure of deposits will not be permitted until completion of 3 months from the date of deposit
    • For deposits foreclosed after 3 months but before 6 months, no interest will be paid
    • For deposits foreclosed after 6 months, interest at 2% will be deducted from the normal rate of Simple Interest for the deposit period

* Terms & conditions apply and all deposit plans are only for members/shareholders.
